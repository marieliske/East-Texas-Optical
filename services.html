Services

Services We Offer

What we can do to help you see!

Repairs & Adjustments

Did you accidently bend your frames out of wack or lose a screw? We can try to fix it! We will work with you to find the best solution!

Same Day Service

With a lab on-site and a wide variety of lenses, we offer same-day service on most prescriptions. No more waiting for weeks for your glasses!

Frame Replacements

What if your frames break but your lenses are in good shape? We can insert your lenses into a new frame!

Lens Replacements

Love your old frames but your prescription changed? You can keep wearing your old glasses until we get your new lenses ready for you. We will cut them into your frame on-site!

Sunglasses

Whether you need prescription or non-prescription lenses, we’ve got you covered. Choose from polarized options to reduce glare or tinted lenses for added comfort. Our sunglasses not only enhance your vision but also provide the ultimate protection against harmful UV rays.

On-Site Independent Optometrist

We have wonderful independent optometrists on site, providing you with personalized and professional eye care, contacts and exams tailored to your specific needs. To contact their office, call (903)-878-2452
